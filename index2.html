About Me

Who I am & what I do

Hey there! I'm a tech enthusiast with a passion for designing and managing network systems, ensuring seamless connectivity, and keeping infrastructure secure. I graduated with a B.Voc in Networking and Mobile Application from PSG College of Arts & Science. Currently, I’m working as a Network Data Administrator – L1 at Wipro Technologies, Coimbatore, through their Work Integrated Learning Program (WILP). Alongside my role, I’m also pursuing an M.Tech in Software Systems from BITS Pilani—because for me, learning and growth never stop.

My expertise lies in network administration, system monitoring, troubleshooting, and cybersecurity practices. I also bring experience in web development, cloud computing, and data analysis, with strong command over languages like C, C++, Java, Python, HTML, PHP, and SQL. Notable projects include a college event management system, a crypto-based e-museum ticketing platform, and a bus ticket booking portal. Beyond technology, I’ve served as an IIC Student Ambassador and Association Chairperson, where I honed leadership, event management, and team collaboration skills.
